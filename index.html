Frequently Asked Questions

What is Synthica?
Synthica is a free research platform built for high school students worldwide. We provide curriculum, community, and competitions to help you launch your research career early.
Do I need prior research experience?
Not at all. Our curriculum starts from the ground up and guides you through every stage of the research process, regardless of your background.
Is Synthica really free?
Yes — 100% free, always. We believe access to research education should not be gated behind tuition or fees.
What subjects can I research?
Any field you're passionate about — physics, biology, computer science, economics, psychology, climate, and more.
How do the competitions work?
After developing your project, you can submit it to our international competitions where judges evaluate your methodology, findings, and presentation.
Can I join if I'm outside the US?
Absolutely. Synthica is a global community with researchers from dozens of countries. Everything is online and open internationally.
